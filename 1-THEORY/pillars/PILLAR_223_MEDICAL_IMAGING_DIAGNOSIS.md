## Scope

This pillar turns imaging and diagnostic reasoning into explicit quantitative tools:

- Ultrasound axial-resolution estimation from frequency and bandwidth
- CT dose risk estimation with age modulation
- Bayesian PPV/NPV computation from prevalence, sensitivity, specificity
- Multi-modality probability fusion
- Triage recommendation logic
- Cross-pillar alignment score coupling safety (sound), sensing quality (nano), and diagnostic certainty

## Why it matters

Medical diagnosis fails when image quality, uncertainty, and clinical action are disconnected. This pillar enforces coherent chains:

1. **Acquire safely** (sound-energy limits),
2. **measure reliably** (nanotech/sensor quality),
3. **infer transparently** (Bayesian + fused evidence),
4. **act proportionally** (triage thresholds).

## Epistemic status

These functions are decision-support heuristics and educational models, not medical-device software or clinical directives.
