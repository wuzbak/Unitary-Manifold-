## Scope

This pillar formalizes sound as both an information carrier and an energy-transfer medium. It provides:

- SPL ↔ pressure conversion and intensity estimation
- Radiation force estimates for acoustic manipulation
- Piezoelectric harvesting estimates
- Ultrasound attenuation and mechanical-index safety checks
- A rigor-focused use matrix linking imaging, therapy, harvesting, and nanocontrol

## Why it matters

Sound couples strongly to matter and can be controlled without ionizing radiation. This makes it central for:

- diagnostics (ultrasound),
- precision actuation (acoustic force fields),
- low-power autonomous sensing (piezoelectric harvesting),
- and cross-domain integration with nanotech and medical diagnosis.

## Epistemic status

The equations implemented are standard acoustics engineering approximations and safety heuristics; they are not claims of new fundamental physics.
