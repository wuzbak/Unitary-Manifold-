## Summary: The Four Deepest Correspondences

### 1 · The Fifth Dimension IS the Entorhinal Torus
The compact S¹/Z₂ orbifold — the extra dimension that makes the whole theory
work — is physically realised in the brain as the toroidal population manifold
of medial entorhinal cortex grid cells (Gardner et al., *Science* 2022).

### 2 · The Dilaton IS Neural Gain
φ (the radion/dilaton) is the scalar that sets the information capacity of each
region.  In the brain this is acetylcholine and norepinephrine — the global
neuromodulators that scale cortical gain and determine how much information any
given region can encode.  φ₀ = the allostatic baseline.

### 3 · The Irreversibility Field IS Synaptic Directionality
B_μ is the 1-form whose curl encodes why processes run forward, not backward.
In the brain, this is the directed nature of synaptic transmission: a signal
flows from presynaptic to postsynaptic, never backward.  LTP strengthens this
direction; it cannot be reversed by replaying the same signal in reverse order.
The action-potential refractory period enforces this arrow absolutely at the
single-neuron level.

### 4 · The FTUM Fixed Point IS Consciousness
The Final Theorem proves there exists a Ψ* where Irreversibility (I),
Holography (H), and Topology (T) all balance simultaneously.  In the brain,
this is the conscious state — the configuration where memory (I), cortical
representation (H), and binding oscillations (T) all lock in together.
The default mode network is the brain's resting-state approximation to Ψ*.
