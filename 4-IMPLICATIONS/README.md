## What this folder is

When the Unitary Manifold was built, it was quickly apparent that the φ-field
geometry — measuring entropic coupling, attractor dynamics, and irreversibility
— has analogical reach far beyond cosmology.  AxiomZero commissioned an
extensive exploration of these implications.  The result is a body of work that
is genuine, careful, and mathematically structured, but which occupies a
different epistemic category from the provable physics.

The framework borrowed from the Unitary Manifold here is the *mathematical
structure* — φ-attractor dynamics, entropy maximisation, irreversibility
measures — not the specific claim that this geometry describes the 5D
compactification of spacetime.  Many of these modules would remain interesting
even if the core Kaluza-Klein claim were falsified.
