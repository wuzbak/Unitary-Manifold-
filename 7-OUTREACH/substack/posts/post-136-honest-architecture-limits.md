## P27: The Strong CP Problem — θ̄ < 10⁻¹⁰

The strong CP problem is one of the deepest fine-tuning puzzles in physics. The QCD Lagrangian admits a CP-violating term parameterized by the angle θ̄. Experimentally, θ̄ < 10⁻¹⁰. Theoretically, there is no reason it should not be of order 1.

**Unitary Manifold status: ARCHITECTURE_LIMIT_CERTIFIED (0.1 points)**

The 5D framework identifies the closing mechanism: a Peccei-Quinn axion, arising from the compactification geometry, dynamically relaxes θ̄ to zero. The axion quality problem — whether the PQ symmetry is sufficiently protected against higher-dimensional operators — is resolved at the architecture level by the discrete symmetries of the T²/Z₃ orbifold.

**What is not done:** Computing the explicit axion mass and coupling constant from first principles within the 5D framework. This requires tracking the PQ symmetry through the full KK tower, a calculation that is identified in scope but not yet executed.

The honest statement: we know the mechanism. We have not computed the prediction.

## P28: The Cosmological Constant — Λ ≈ 2.89×10⁻¹²² M_Pl⁴

The cosmological constant problem is worse. The observed dark energy density is about 10⁻¹²² times the Planck density. Quantum field theory naively predicts it should be of order 1. The discrepancy is 122 orders of magnitude — the largest known discrepancy between theory and observation in all of physics.

**Unitary Manifold status: ARCHITECTURE_LIMIT_CERTIFIED (10D) (0.1 points)**

**What is not done:** Closing the remaining 10⁵⁸ factor requires the full 10D or 11D completion of the Unitary Manifold — the embedding of the 5D geometry into a string-theoretic or M-theoretic framework with complete flux stabilization.

The honest statement: this is an architecture problem. The 5D framework can reduce the problem but cannot solve it within its own scope.

## Why These Are Different From the Other Open Items

P16 (solar mass splitting) and P26 (neutrino absolute mass scale) are also not fully closed. But they are CONSTRAINED at 0.5 points — meaning the 5D geometry makes a prediction that is in the right ballpark, and the remaining gap has an identified geometric source.

This distinction is important. CONSTRAINED means "we are close but not certified." ARCHITECTURE_LIMIT_CERTIFIED means "we understand why the 5D framework cannot close this, and we know what would be required."
